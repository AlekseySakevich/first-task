Eddie Redmayne and Birdman pull ahead in Oscars race

Eddie Redmayne pulled ahead of his rivals in the race for the best actor Oscar when he won a Screen
Actor's Guild award for his role as Stephen Hawking in The Theory of Everything on Sunday night. The
32-year-old actor beat rivals Benedict Cumberbatch, Steve Carell, Jake Gyllenhaal and Michael Keaton to
the prize for male actor in a leading role at the ceremony in Los Angeles.
